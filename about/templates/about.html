About Me.

Hi there, I'm Kieren, it's nice to meet you. Here's a little more information about me.

What I do

Right now I work as an application developer in the online marketing space, focussing on data analysis and interactive development. I have worked in the web space since 2011 and I genuinely enjoy what I do.

I am versed in HTML5 and CSS3, Sass/SCSS, Python (Flask, Django), PHP (Slim, Synfony, and a little Zend), JavaScript (React, JQuery), Git, Linux (debian based), Postgresql and MySql, Elasticsearch, DNS management (in AWS Route53), Apache and Nginx, Docker, and a little golang.

My Education

I graduated high school at Cornwall Hill College in Pretoria in 2008, and studied a BA in Interactive Development from The Open Window University, from 2011 to 2015 also in Pretoria.

Personally

I live in London, United Kingdom, but I grew up in Gauteng, sunny South Africa. I moved between Johannesburg and Pretoria until I emigrated to the UK to be with family in 2016.

I can speak English, Afrikaans, a little Northern Sotho, and am learning Portuguese from my fiance, Patricia.

I enjoy go-karting, watching Formula 1, knitting, playing cricket, golfing on occasion, playing PC games with my friends, and reading articles on space and physics. I also moderate a group that posts dad jokes on Scooploop.

In 2019 I founded a start up called Carderbee, a Comparison Shopping Service - or CSS - that compares the prices of products from various different merchants based in the United Kingdom.
